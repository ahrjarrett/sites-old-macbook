Keep Your Pool In Tip-Top Shape

At Sunray Pools, we offer any and all services to make sure your swimming pool and spa look and run their best. Services include:

  • Weekly Pool Cleaning Service
  • Emergency repairs or water chemistry adjustment and analysis
  • Factory authorized repairs on all pool and spa components
  • Upgrades (Salt-water systems, heat pumps, ozonators, etc.)
  • Renovations & Remodels
  • Acid wash & stain removal

 

When you schedule any of the above services with us, you will get a courtesy confirmation call letting you know when our uniformed technician will arrive at your home. In most cases, you do not need to be there for us to perform our services.

 

Our fees are reasonable, and all our work is guaranteed. For more information, or to schedule service, please call 972.333.0714 or fill out the form below.

 

Weekly Pool Cleaning Service

We provide a weekly pool cleaning & chemical service so you can have a truly “Maintenance–Free” pool.  We don’t require an annual contract, but if you sign up for one, we’ll offer a 10% discount right up front.

 

Each weekly cleaning visit includes:

  • Vacuum the pool
  • Brush the walls & tiles.
  • Empty the skimmer baskets
  • Clean or backwash the filter
  • Empty the pump & filter bask
  • Skim away any surface debris
  • Test and adjust the  chemicals
  • Leave a service ticket, reporting what we’ve done
